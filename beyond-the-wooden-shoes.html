Beyond wooden shoes

Twelve years surrounded by Indian culture, Indian friends and Indian colleagues, made me beyond being Dutch. Living in India for the first six years and then travelling in en out for my work for another six, made me coming back to the Netherland, but not coming home. It was more "starting over" with a complete new perspective, no longer stuck in the one of my Dutch culture. As in an adventure, finding a way again. Being someone else and yet again not. What it means, feels like and what I am experiences I'd like share in my blog "Beyond Wooden Shoes", as well as topics that occupy me in every day life, whether that's in The Netherlands or elsewhere in the world, always from a perspective that I've developed in the years I was in India.
